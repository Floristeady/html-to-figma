Built on Microsoft Soundscape Technology

10 years of research into 3D spatial audio cues

  • Originally developed to help people with low vision
  • Rich 3D spatial audio for navigation assistance
  • Accessibility technology brought to mainstream
  • Led by the original Microsoft project team

Soundscape was a 10-year research project undertaken by Microsoft, set up to investigate how rich 3D spatial audio cues can help people with low vision gain more mobility independence.

Important Safety and Usage Disclaimer

This prototype product is designed to enhance spatial awareness and assist navigation through immersive auditory cues. However, it should not be relied upon as the sole means of orientation or wayfinding. Environmental hazards, unexpected obstacles, or dynamic conditions may not be fully conveyed through sound cues alone. Users are responsible for ensuring safe and informed use in accordance with personal needs and situational context.

The mapping data incorporated into the SSGC program is captured from a third-party program, and therefore, there may be limitations with the accuracy of the information presented and the user is responsible for their actions and their own safety and well-being.

By using this product, you acknowledge that reliance on auditory cues alone may increase risk in certain situations, and that the developers disclaim liability for accidents or injuries arising from such use.
